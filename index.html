AI language coach

Learn languages through your own story world.

StoryLine is a personalized AI language coach for iOS. Each language pair lives in a persistent story universe with missions, flipcards, and structured Foundation practice — taught in your mother tongue.

Story missions

Daily episodes with dialogue, listening, recall, and writing in an ongoing narrative.

Foundation coach

Structured skill practice for vocabulary, grammar, listening, reading, and production.

Your progress

CEFR-aligned levels, spaced-repetition flipcards tied to scenes you lived, and sync across devices.

Account deletion

How to delete your account

  1. Open StoryLine and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

This permanently deletes your account and all associated cloud learning data. Local app data is also cleared. Deletion cannot be undone.

If deletion fails, email fw@kirmes.app.

So löschst du dein Konto

  1. StoryLine öffnen und anmelden.
  2. Profil → Einstellungen öffnen.
  3. Konto löschen tippen und bestätigen.

Konto und alle Cloud-Lerndaten werden dauerhaft gelöscht. Lokale Daten ebenfalls. Endgültig.
