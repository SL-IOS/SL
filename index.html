Data controller

StoryLine · Copenhagen, Denmark · fstoryline@icloud.com

1. What we collect

  • Account data: Sign in with Apple or Google Sign-In.
  • Learning data: language pairs, CEFR level, progress, flipcards, coach history, exercise results.
  • Optional contact data: email and phone if provided in the app.
  • Generated content: AI-generated story chapters, exercises, and coach cards.
  • Device and usage data: technical data to operate and improve the app.
  • Advertising: with consent, via Google AdMob and App Tracking Transparency on iOS.
  • Notifications: device token if you allow push notifications.

2. How we use data

  • Provide and personalize language coaching and story content.
  • Sync progress across sessions and devices.
  • Operate authentication, cloud storage, and AI content generation.
  • Show ads according to your consent settings.
  • Respond to support requests and legal obligations.

3. Legal basis (GDPR)

Contract performance, consent, legitimate interests, and legal obligations.

4. Storage

Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions) and Google Cloud / Vertex AI for AI features. Where possible, data is kept in the EU.

5. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Sharing only with service providers under contract or when required by law.

6. Retention

Data is kept only as long as needed. You can delete your account in the app (see Account deletion).

7. Your rights

Access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, withdraw consent: fstoryline@icloud.com.

8. Children

For users who can lawfully consent in their jurisdiction.

  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
